# Play Store Listing - Chess Master Offline

## Short Description

Play chess offline with local multiplayer, AI, undo, and saved games.

## Full Description

Chess Master Offline is a clean, privacy-friendly chess game made for Android players who want classic chess without login, internet, or distractions.

Play against a friend on the same device or challenge the built-in AI across Easy, Medium, and Hard levels. The app validates legal moves, highlights available moves, tracks move history, supports undo, and saves your current game locally so you can resume later.

Designed for quick casual games and focused practice, Chess Master Offline includes a polished board UI, multiple board themes, pawn promotion, castling, en passant, check, checkmate, stalemate, and draw detection.

No account required. No internet required. No unnecessary permissions.

## Privacy Policy Draft

Chess Master Offline is an offline chess game. The app does not require users to create an account and does not request internet, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or storage permissions.

The app stores user settings and saved chess game state locally on the user's device using Android DataStore. This local data may include board theme, piece style, sound preference, AI difficulty, current board position, and move history.

Chess Master Offline does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The current release does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, social login, or payment processing.

If future versions add optional ads, analytics, cloud sync, or premium purchases, this privacy policy should be updated before release to describe any new data practices.
